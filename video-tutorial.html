Accessing On Demand Videos

To view the videos on this channel, you have three options.

Buy access to individual videos or video series

Price varies per series

This is the least economical option if you plan on watching a lot of videos but it might serve your purpose if you are only interested in a few videos or one series.

To purchase videos this way, simply click on the
Purchase the Series
buttons next to each video above.

Become a Ladybug Tools Supporter

$25 per month

With this, you will get access to all of the videos above and all future videos that we produce. We make a new series each month and, if you plan on watching more than 2-3 of them, this will likely be more economical than buying each individually.

To become a Ladybug Tools Supporter, click on the "Become a Supporter" button below and follow the instructions at the bottom of this page. You can then access videos by clicking on the
Membership Login
buttons next to each video above.

Become a Supporter!

Convince your school or company to become a Ladybug Tools Partner

Free for all students / employees of the partner school / company

This is an annual commitment on the part of your school / company and will ensure that all students or employees get access to all of the videos above and all future videos that we produce over the course of the year.

To apply, click one of the two buttons below. If you already belong to a partner organization, follow the instructions at the bottom of this page.

Become an Instituional Partner! Get Company-Wide Video Access!

Already a Supporter or with a Partner?

You're so close!
To access the videos via the Membership Login buttons above, you need to follow these steps:

  • Create an account on Vimeo that has the same email address as your Patreon account. If you are with a partner, make sure that your account email address is with your company or school's domain.
  • Email vimeo@ladybug.tools and state your vimeo account's email address and your Vimeo ID in the email. Your Vimeo ID is the name that appears after "https://vimeo.com/" on your profile page. Typically, this is "user########" with a random stream of numbers for new Vimeo users.
  • You will recieve an email within 3 days telling you that you have been invited to the Ladybug Tools Supporters Group. Follow the link in the email to join the group.
  • After you join the group, the Membership login buttons above will take you right to the videos whenever you are logged into your Vimeo account.
