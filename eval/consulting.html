Services

As the creators of Ladybug Tools, we can help you make best use of our software in your business by providing on-site training, custom tools or scripts, and project consulting. To request any of the following services simply contact us!


Training

From the IBPSA conference, to ACADIA, to private office workshops, we have trained designers, engineers and building simulation experts alike. We pride ourselves on our ability to teach to different levels of expertise and we tailor our training material to ensure it meets your audience's needs. Check our workshop packages, upcoming workshop or hire us for a workshop at your office or conference.

Custom Tools and Workflows

Do you have an idea for a new tool, workflow, or simulation but aren't sure how to make it a reality? We can help build it, whether it's a new Grasshopper/Dynamo component, a script, or a web interface. We have worked on a variety of online tools including DesignExplorer, EPWMap, and the Glazing and Winter Comfort Tool. We can help you to build yours!

Project Consulting

We have helped many people use Ladybug Tools on a variety of projects and at different scales. Oftentimes, this is simply by answering questions on our forum but, because this forum-based help is all supported by volunteers, we can't always guarantee a timely response that fixes your issues. For this reason, if you need continuous support let us know and we can help you as a consultant.

Cloud Computing

We are currently in the process of developing a cloud service for Ladybug Tools. The service will support automated daylight simulation, energy modeling, and CFD analysis and will be optimized for Ladybug Tools workflows. It will offer significant time savings over the current locally-run simulations. Stay tuned!
